## What this wiki is for

This site is our single place for design practice at NJIA. It explains how we approach research, content, design systems, enablement, and leadership so the teams we support can move faster with more confidence and better outcomes.

## What we believe

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Design for everyone" icon="heart">
    Good government services work for every New Jerseyan — regardless of device, language, ability, or familiarity with digital tools.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Write with clarity" icon="document">
    We choose plain language and clear structure so people can find, understand, and act on information without extra friction.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build in public" icon="comment-alt">
    Shared documentation, open collaboration, and visible decisions help teams stay aligned and make stronger products faster.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use evidence, not opinion" icon="magnifier">
    Research and data guide our choices. We speak to people, test ideas, and learn continuously from real service users.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Make systems matter" icon="puzzle">
    We design components, patterns, and workflows that work across teams and services, not just on one page.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Support our partners" icon="approve-check-circle">
    We coach, enable, and partner with teammates so they can own and sustain great service experiences over time.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
