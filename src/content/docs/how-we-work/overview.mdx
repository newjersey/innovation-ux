# How We Work

This section explains how our team operates — our purpose, guiding principles, and the processes we use to plan, run, and share work.

## Purpose

We deliver user-centered services by combining research, design, content, and operational coordination. Our work focuses on clarity, accessibility, and measurable outcomes.

## Principles

- Center residents: design for real people and their needs.
- Iterate quickly: low-cost prototypes and rapid learning.
- Collaborate across teams: involve policy, engineering, and operations early.
- Be inclusive: prioritize accessibility and language access.

## Process

1. Define goals and success criteria.
2. Recruit participants and run lightweight research.
3. Prototype solutions and test with users.
4. Iterate, document findings, and hand off to implementation.

## Roles & Responsibilities

- Researchers: plan studies, recruit participants, synthesize findings.
- Designers: create prototypes, run usability sessions, and refine interaction design.
- Content designers: write plain-language content and ensure language access.
- Product and program owners: make decisions, prioritize work, and secure resources.
