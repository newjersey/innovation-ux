## Why design principles?

Our design principles are the foundation for everything we build. They help new team members understand how we work, give us a shared language with partners and stakeholders, and give us something concrete to refer back to when tough calls need to be made on a project.

## Our principles

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Inclusive and accessible" icon="star">
    We prioritize people who have historically lacked access to services — in how we choose projects and how we execute them. Regardless of language, device access, economic status, ability, location, age, or digital literacy, New Jerseyans should be able to access, understand, and participate in government services.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Simplicity and standardization" icon="list-format">
    We champion plain language, ease of use, and seamless access to services. We value standardization over reinventing the wheel and look for opportunities to systematize improvements so the whole organization benefits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build with, not for" icon="open-book">
    We learn from and co-create with the people who live and work in New Jersey — early and often. We use both qualitative and quantitative research methods to understand deeply and broadly how people engage with government services.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Iterate and collaborate" icon="puzzle">
    Product development is a team sport. We involve teammates and stakeholders as early as possible, starting simple — sketches, then wireframes, then full designs — collecting feedback from real users at every stage, including after release.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Measure twice, cut once" icon="approve-check-circle">
    Speed means nothing if something isn't used, and solving one problem while creating another isn't success. We deliver incremental solutions in a timely way, but always consider them in context. We think in systems to ensure our work is relevant, feasible, viable, scalable, and sustainable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Elevate and coach" icon="rocket">
    Within our team, we hold a high bar for design excellence. With partners, we meet them where they are and bring them into the design process — building shared capability across the organization over time.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
